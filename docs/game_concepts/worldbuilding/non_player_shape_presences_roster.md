# Urchin Game Concept: Non-Player Shape Presences Roster

**Status:** Planning roster  
**Date:** 2026-04-24  
**Purpose:** Define sparse, meaningful non-player presences for the game without turning the world into a conventional NPC cast.

---

## 1. Design Rule

These are not standard NPCs.

They are:
- presences,
- witnesses,
- pilgrims,
- custodians,
- threshold beings,
- carriers,
- omens.

They should be understood through:
- motion,
- context,
- traces,
- symbolic association,
- behavior.

Not dialogue.

---

## 2. Roster Principles

- Keep the total number small.
- Recur sparingly.
- Give each presence a clear movement philosophy.
- Let each one feel partially ambiguous.
- Avoid making them mascot-like.
- Preserve the urchin as the emotional center.

---

## 3. Presence 1: The Meander Pilgrim

### Base Form

A sphericon-like or asymmetrical rolling form.

### Primary Meaning

- pilgrimage
- rhythm
- indirect progress
- guidance through path, not instruction

### How It Appears

- glimpsed at a distance crossing dunes or ridges
- seen briefly at impossible angles
- never encountered as a standard interaction partner
- often gone by the time the player reaches where it was

### What It Leaves Behind

- meandering traces in sand
- offset resonance lines
- looping route suggestions
- glyph clusters associated with asymmetrical progress

### World Function

This presence teaches the player:
- not all progress is linear,
- beautiful routes may not be direct,
- other forms exist that inhabit the world differently.

### Best Biome Placement

- Sand Choir
- Marble Dunes
- possibly The Inner Sea as a final echo

---

## 4. Presence 2: The Facet Keeper

### Base Form

A tricylinder-like or tri-mode rolling entity.

### Primary Meaning

- thresholds
- reorientation
- multiple stable truths
- transition through chosen state

### How It Appears

- near gates, pivots, or route branches
- sometimes dormant like architecture
- sometimes visibly rotates or shifts mode at key moments
- more still than the Meander Pilgrim

### What It Leaves Behind

- tri-radial glyphs
- pivot marks
- threshold circles
- path triples where one route becomes correct only after state change

### World Function

This presence teaches:
- some places require changing how you move,
- certain problems are solved by reorientation, not persistence,
- thresholds are sacred and real.

### Best Biome Placement

- Glass Forest
- threshold chambers between major biomes
- Wind Arches approach zones

---

## 5. Presence 3: The Bloom Listener

### Base Form

A soft, partially floral or membrane-based rounded being, more implied than seen clearly.

### Primary Meaning

- listening
- gentleness
- organic resonance
- call and response

### How It Appears

- hidden in Bloom Caverns
- perceived through synchronized pulsing structures
- maybe glimpsed as silhouette behind membranes
- not necessarily confirmed as separate from the environment

### What It Leaves Behind

- pulsing radial bloom patterns
- calm resonance zones
- softly repeating glyphs in living tissue

### World Function

This presence teaches:
- the world can listen back,
- resonance is relational, not merely instrumental,
- gentleness matters.

### Best Biome Placement

- Bloom Caverns
- select Moss Cathedral sanctuaries

---

## 6. Presence 4: The Mirror Skimmer

### Base Form

A low, elegant, elongated or flattened reflective form capable of gliding over shallow water or reflective surfaces.

### Primary Meaning

- serenity
- surface reading
- grace
- relation between momentum and reflection

### How It Appears

- crossing mirror pools in the distance
- leaving almost calligraphic wakes
- sometimes awakening as a rideable or symbolic precursor form

### What It Leaves Behind

- reflected route patterns
- wake glyphs
- shimmer trails
- crossing arcs

### World Function

This presence foreshadows:
- water skimming,
- broader graceful motion,
- the idea that the world can be crossed lightly rather than forcefully.

### Best Biome Placement

- River Mirrors
- The Inner Sea

---

## 7. Presence 5: The Seed Ascendant

### Base Form

A glider-like or seed-sail form, possibly a being, vehicle, or dormant organism depending on context.

### Primary Meaning

- lift
- freedom
- trust
- late-game transformation

### How It Appears

- seen carried by strong thermals
- attached to wind structures
- awakened through resonance rather than “owned”
- only fully understood late in the game

### What It Leaves Behind

- uplift arc glyphs
- falling seed trails
- wind alignment marks
- suspended geometry echoes

### World Function

This presence foreshadows:
- vehicle-assisted ascent,
- sail/glide mastery,
- the game’s end-state of freer motion.

### Best Biome Placement

- Wind Arches
- The Inner Sea

---

## 8. Presence 6: The Great Carrier

### Base Form

A giant dormant rolling or channel-like organism/structure that blurs creature and transport.

### Primary Meaning

- scale
- trust
- being carried by the world
- movement larger than the self

### How It Appears

- first as landscape,
- later as awakened transit,
- maybe only once or twice as a true event.

### What It Leaves Behind

- massive route carvings
- conductor glyphs
- resonant path channels
- evidence that whole environments once moved

### World Function

This presence enables:
- one or two major set pieces,
- a sense of ancient mobility at world scale,
- emotional transition from solitary movement to cooperative passage.

### Best Biome Placement

- late Bloom Caverns
- River Mirrors
- The Inner Sea

---

## 9. Presence 7: The Impossible Form

### Base Form

A rare revelation-state entity or geometry that may not be fully representable under ordinary physics.

### Primary Meaning

- hidden order
- transcendence
- unreadable truth made briefly visible

### How It Appears

- only in visionary interludes,
- never fully in normal play,
- perhaps as layered shape, impossible symmetry, or moving glyph-body hybrid.

### What It Leaves Behind

- unified glyph systems
- impossible geometry glimpses
- memory traces across multiple biomes

### World Function

This presence is not a routine being. It is a cosmological glimpse.

Use it carefully.

### Best Biome Placement

- Bloom Caverns revelation
- Wind Arches revelation
- possibly final Inner Sea climax

---

## 10. Roster Summary

| Presence | Main Shape Logic | Main Story Role |
|---|---|---|
| Meander Pilgrim | sphericon-like asymmetry | distant guide, path philosophy |
| Facet Keeper | tricylinder / tri-mode | thresholds, reorientation |
| Bloom Listener | soft radial organic form | listening world, relational resonance |
| Mirror Skimmer | low reflective glide form | serenity, surface fluency |
| Seed Ascendant | sail / glider seed form | uplift, late-game freedom |
| Great Carrier | giant dormant transit-being | world-scale cooperation |
| Impossible Form | revelation geometry-being | hidden order, transcendence |

---

## 11. Recommendation

Use at most:
- 3 clearly recurring presences,
- 2 occasional large-form events,
- 1 revelation-only impossible presence.

Strong default set:
- Meander Pilgrim
- Facet Keeper
- Seed Ascendant
- optional Bloom Listener
- one Great Carrier event

That is enough to give the world company, mystery, and symbolic depth without crowding the urchin’s emotional role.
